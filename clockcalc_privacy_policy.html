Privacy Policy for ClockCalc

Last updated: 7 July 2026

ClockCalc is designed to be a simple time calculation app. This Privacy Policy explains how ClockCalc handles information when you use the app.

Information We Collect

ClockCalc does not collect, store, share, sell, or transmit any personal information.

The app does not require you to create an account, sign in, or provide personal details.

How the App Works

ClockCalc allows users to calculate time-related problems, such as adding or subtracting hours and minutes, calculating end times, and working out durations.

Any information entered into the app is used only on your device for the purpose of performing the calculation.

Data Storage

ClockCalc does not upload your calculations or any personal data to external servers.

Any calculation data entered into the app remains on your device only while using the app, unless a future version clearly states otherwise.

Third-Party Services

ClockCalc does not use third-party analytics, advertising networks, tracking tools, or external data collection services.

Children’s Privacy

ClockCalc does not knowingly collect personal information from children or any other users.

Internet Access

ClockCalc does not require internet access to perform its core time calculation features.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated “Last updated” date.

Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, you can contact the app developer through the support contact provided on the App Store listing.
